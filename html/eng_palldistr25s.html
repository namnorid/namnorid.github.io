Pallet distributor 2 5S

Responsible: Daniel Namnori
Assignment: Pallet distribution
Section: HUB – Production – Pallets
Process: Pallet distribution
Updated: 2019-09-23
5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain om
Equipment

-  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

-  Communication equipment: Motorola walkie talkie marked with number 14. The Motorola walkie talkie is charged at the main office.

-  Computers: Four computers for general use in the office.

-  Forklift: Counterbalance forklift marked with Pallet distributor 1, which stands at regular forklift loading. Replacement batteries are available if needed. Change the forklift battery prior reaching 20 %. Cleaning the forklift from general debris after production. Water the forklift if the battery power exceeds 90 %. After production, the forklift should be put on charge. If the forklift is broken, inform the forklift manager Pontus Nero about the type of problem the forklift has and put a note on the forklift that the forklift is damaged and must not be used. Ask the nearest manager about which forklift can be used temporarily until the regular forklift is repaired.

-  Other equipment: Ballpoint pen, knife, marking pen (fig. 2.)

-  If other equipment is missing, contact Team leader.

Workstation

The primary workstation is the pallet square, which is located outside gates 45-50 of the terminal. Open the terminal doors by placing your card against the card reader and entering your password if required. General rubbish should be put in the trash cans at the doors. If rails from pallets are on the terminal floor, they must be removed and placed between the gates and preferably in the trash cans. The wheels of the trucks will be severely worn if we do not remove them. The pallets may only be placed within each area due to the risk of poor visibility.

Cleaning

After production, clean the area you have been assigned on the Cleaning position list. Cleaning zones may vary, always check the cleaning list.
Daily cleaning on cleaning zone 8 (fig. 1) ’Pallet crusher’:

1.  Sort out intact EU pallets and disposable pallets.

2.  Broken pallets are thrown in the pallet crusher outside gate 12/13 (though not at slippery road conditions. Is then placed in the gatehouse and Team leader is notified.)

3.  Pack the pallet to be thrown in fine stacks.

4.  Sweep the area around the forklift charge area.

5.  Wrap the corrugated cardboard together and throw them in the well container.

6.  Stack the intact pallets and place outside of gate 12. At more than 6 stacks, drive the remaining stacks to the pallet area in the North hall.

7.  Help others with their respective cleaning areas.

Cleaning every week: Clean behind the yellow collision protections.
