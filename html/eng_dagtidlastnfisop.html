Finland loading daytime SOP

Responsible: Sofie Wahlström
Assignment: Loading Finland daytime
Section: HUB – Production – Pallets
Process: Export departures daytime
Updated: 2019-09-27
SOP: Standard Operating Procedure
Start

Begin by collecting the equipment listed in 5S.

Ordinary work

The work consists of unloading DKE-SEJ 1 with arrival 12:45 and load JKP-FIH 1 with departure 14:00.

•      Prioritize the 24-hour customers: Shimano, Toshiba, Tura and Aurora, and booked LQ goods.

1.      It’s important to check the email from the Traffic Department (booking.se@bring.com) with the title: 'LQ Krisas' and see what LQ goods (1) is booked to be loaded at JKP-FIH 1.

•      Make sure that the LQ goods that have been booked are loaded and be sure to load it regarding the loading order.

•      Note the number of full pallets and split pallets from the 24h customers.

2.      Load JKP-FIH 1 with the following loading order: Posti (B2C), Bring-Vantaa (B2B), Turku and Tampere (B2B).

•      Prioritize goods for Vantaa.

•      Load the goods from Finland sorting outermost, but so the remaining load order is followed.

•      Prioritized goods must be shipped but deprioritize Toshiba's full pallets if needed.

3.      Print CMRs if not already done; see SOP for Daytime 1.

•      On the CMR to Turku, the plomb number must also be stated.

4.      Write load list in MyBooking or use Mall Danmark (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Lastlistor\Sverige\Danmark), but do not save this.

a.      In MyBooking → click on the truck icon → search in white field: ”SEH-FIH 1” → click on the plus icon to the right (Create dispatch).

b.      Specify the 24h customers and fill in a line for full pallets, as well as one for packages, per customer.

•      Since it is not a load order on Fridays, the 24h customers must still be specified on the loading list.

5.      Add customers who are not already in the list by clicking the plus icon (Departure.) → Click on the notepad at the top right (Loading list) to save.

•      NOTE, check that it is added to the correct car / tab.

•      Supplement Excel File as needed and hide empty rows, i.e. customers without goods.

6.      Print 3 copies, sign and give to the driver along with the CMRs. NOTE, on CMR for Turku, specify the number of pallets, not the number of pallets.

•      Do not save the Excel file; only close.

7.      After the JKP-FIH 1 is sent, note customers and count the number of remaining Finland.

8.      Fill in the daily report (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Lastlistor\Sverige\Dagrapport).

a.      Fill in customer, pallet, pallet places, any LQ goods (incl. label date.)

b.      In the Övrig info box, enter the number of full pallets + split pallets for 24h customers.

c.      Press Ctrl Shift U, write deviations and send the email.

9.      Fill in arrival for DKE-SEJ 1 and departure time for JKP-FIH 1 in the traffic report (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Jönköping\PR Hub\Nattrapport\Trafikrapport…)

Notices

•      No sorting if DKE-SEJ 1 arrives after 13:20.

•      No sorting or loading order on Fridays.

End of production

Clean up the workstation you were working on.
