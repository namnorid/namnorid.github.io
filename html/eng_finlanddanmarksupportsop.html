Finland / Denmark support SOP

Responsible: Vipin P. Jay
Assignment: Support loading Finland / Danmark export
Section: HUB – Production – Pallets
Process: Loading FI/DK and departures
Updated: 2019-10-22
SOP: Standard Operating Procedure
Start

Begin by picking up the equipment specified in 5S. After the pre-production meeting, go to gate 6-9 (see Figure 1) and assist Export Loader FI/DK.

Ordinary work

1.Load the departure for Kolding (SEJ-DKE 7) with departure time at 20:30.

a. Note down the customers, which are to be loaded into the trailer.
b. Load the pallets with the customers written into the trailer.
c. Affix the plomb after closing the gates and note down the Plomb number.
d. Go to the computer assigned for the FI / DK loader near the OE station (see 5S for Export loader FI / DK) and open the file for the Denmark departures (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Lastlistor\Sverige).
e. Fill in the Excel sheet for “SEJ-DKW4 Förskott natt” with the appropriate time, date, plomb number, customer name with pallet count, total space occupied in the carrier, followed by signing the sheet with your name.
f. Take two print-outs of the excel sheet with the data printed on one side of the paper.

2.Continue loading the remnant Kolding pallets on the second Kolding car, i.e. SEJ-DKW 1.

a. Unload the Kolding pallets from the Dustin 1 car (ROS-JKP 1) and load it simultaneously into the aforesaid car which will have the car in gate 43 and the trailer in gate 45 unless otherwise specified by the Finland-Denmark Loader.
b. Follow steps 1a to 1d.
c. Fill in the data written down on the Excel sheet labelled “SEJ-DKW1 (Kolding 1 natt)” with the appropriate details as given in step 1e.
d. Follow steps 1f to 1h.

3.Continue in this way and accompany the Finland-Denmark Loader with the loading and unloading help he needs for the remaining carriers to Finland and Denmark until 22:15, after which step 4 may be carried out unless otherwise specified by the Coordinator or the Team leader.

4.Check the Halmstad buffer and preload the trailer, which will be positioned in the gate specified for Halmstad (gate 45,) if the buffer happens to be full and is visibly hampering the smooth flow of production. Load the trailer with high pallets first.

5.Repeat Step 4 for Gävle, Örebro, Västerås (if unloaded) and Borlänge in gates 46-49.

6.Enquire with the coordinator (Unloader 1) as to where help is required, be it loading, unloading or driving pallets.

7.Towards 01:00 start by collecting pallets and cages for destinations labelled 82, 83, 85, 89, 90, 93 and 97. Place them in the respective destinations.

8.Wait until after the break to start loading the Umeå trailer, starting with pallets labelled 90 and going to the pallets labelled 89. Always remember to leave enough places for the pallets labelled 82 as they must go on the aforesaid car. Always remember to be in frequent contact with the FF area in the North Hall on channel 7 since the car loads over there. If they require more pallets or have an excess of pallets, remember to do the needful.

9.The point that must be kept in mind is that the pallets labelled 90 must be furthest on the back side, followed by the pallets labelled to 89, 85, 97, 83, 93. These goes only if space permits. And finally, 82 which is a must-go. The Umeå carrier departs at 02:15 and requires NO CMR.

10.Drive the remnant pallets, avoiding the pallets labelled 82, to the gate where the Gävle 2 (JKP-GVL 2) carrier is docked. This must be done before 02:15 with the help of the pallet distributors and therefore a proper planning of the Umeå car must be done in clear time.

11.Enquire with the coordinator as to where help is required after doing the aforesaid; be it with loading, unloading late cars or driving produced pallets.

End of production

Once all the trucks have departed, go to the office and check what cleaning area you received. Thoroughly clean the area according to the cleaning instructions. Do not forget to return the equipment used during the night (Walkie talkies, forklift etc.) Ask cleaning supervisor where needed help.
