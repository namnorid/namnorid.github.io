Responsible: Daniel Namnori

Equipment

-  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform and reflex vest on the terminal yard. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed.

-  Communication equipment: Motorola walkie-talkie marked with number 25. The Motorola walkie-talkie is charged in the office.

-  Computers: Left computer is for the control of the Beam/gates for entrance to the yard. Right computer is for use with Citrix system, so we use this as an ordinary computer with access to filesystem and applications. If the computers have restarted contact a Team leader to login.

-  Forklift: Non available.

-  Other equipment: Ballpoint pen, knife, marker, bolt cutter.

-  If other equipment is missing, please contact Team leader.

Workstation

The primary workstation is at the main entrance of the terminal area (fig. 1.) Open the door by placing the card on the card reader and entering the password. All the seals are thrown in a box just to the left, plain debris should be disposed of in the dustbin to the right (see picture of the workstation.) For a good working environment, the workstation is equipped with a microwave and coffee machine.

Cleaning

Daily cleaning (see fig. 2 how tidy the area should look like):

1.All rubbish is thrown in the trash.

2.Use the floor mop / brush to clean the floor.

3.Close all windows and turn off the TV.

4.Put the Motorola Walkie Talkie on charge.

5.Go to the main office and fill in the Linehaul report.

6.When the Linehaul Report is completed and saved after normal production, help others complete their cleaning areas.

Cleaning every week: Empty the box with seals in the metal container and empty the bin in combustible garbage.
