Equipment

  -  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket, if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

  -  Communication equipment: No need.

  -  Computers: One computer between chute 26 & 28 (fig 1.)

  -  Forklift: High lift forklift marked with Pallet handler, located at the forklift recharge station. Replacement batteries are available if required. Make sure to change the battery prior reaching 20%. Clean the truck from garbage after production. Water the truck if battery power exceeds 90%. After production, the forklift must be charged. If the forklift is broken, notify Pontus Nero of which type of damage have occurred and attach a damage-flag to the forklift to indicate that the forklift is damaged and may not be used if the damage is severe. Ask Team Leader which forklift to use if the primary forklift is unavailable.

  -  Other equipment: ballpoint pen, knife, marker, notepad.

  -  If equipment is missing contact your Supervisor.

Workstation

The primary workstation is at gates 44-45 and the short side of the sorting machine, at the terminal. See SOP for more info.

  •  Open the doors on the terminal by placing your card against the card reader and enter your password if necessary.

  •  The gates should be litter free. If garbage from pallets is located on the terminal floor, these must be removed and placed between the gatehouses and preferably in the trash baskets. The wheels of the forklifts will wear down if we do not keep the floor clean.

  •  The pallets may only be placed in each specific area due to the risk of poor visibility.

Cleaning

After production, you clean your workstation and the forklift you used during the work shift. Cleaning agents are available if needed.
