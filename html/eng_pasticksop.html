Forwarder SOP

Responsible: Nicholas Kroon
Assignment: Package forwarding
Section: HUB – Production – Packages
Process: Package sorting
Updated: 2019-09-24
SOP: Standard Operating Procedure
Start

Begin with collecting your equipment shown in the 5S. After the preproduction meeting, drive to the sorting machine (fig. 1.) Start placing split goods at the Infeeds, empty cages for EMB volumes and make sure the plastic compressor is emptied.

Ordinary work

Set up goods for Infeed 1, 2, 3 and 4. Pallets and cages is placed 4 in width to allow space for unwrapping plastic from the split pallets and give Infeed personnel enough room to pick packages from the side of the pallets.

1.      Infeed 1 is at ’the Triangle’ where only split pallets are produced (not split cages.) Personnel at Infeed 1 primarily collect their own pallets; Forwarder and Unloaders help when needed. Pallet stacks which are full or cages with EMB goods are moved and placed at the respective place for pallet stacks and EMB goods.

2.      Infeed 2 and 3 primarily produce split goods on pallets, but also split cages if needed. When the pallet is empty, a new split pallet with goods is placed on top of the empty one; max 3 pallets in height. At 3 empty pallets stacked, these pallets should be moved and stacked with a max of 15 pallets in height. When a pallet stack is complete, these are placed at the pallet square (fig. 2.)

3.      Infeed 4 primarily produce split cages. Provide cages with goods and when the cages are empty, move them to the cage compartments between the chutes.

4.      Empty cages shall also be set up at respective Infeed to collect EMB goods. At full cage, move it to the EMB station and replace it with an empty cage.

5.      When goods are retrieved from the split square (fig. 3,) the goods shall be taken in a row from the edges and in. When that is done, you start from the edges again.

6.      Broken pallets are collected and placed in a pile between Infeed 1 and 2. When the pile is sufficiently filled, it is the Forwarder’s task to place these in the designated area at gate 12.

7.      When all Infeeds are full of split goods, the Forwarder helps to unwrap split pallets and adjust pallet stacks.

End of production

When the package sorting is complete, the Forwarder collects the packages under the chutes, i.e. the problem packages, and gives to the Reject station for correction. Once these packages have been collected, start cleaning Infeed 1-4. Clean the area according to the cleaning instructions. Do not forget to return the equipment used during production. Ask the Cleaning supervisor where help is needed.
