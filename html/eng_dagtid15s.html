Daytime 1 5S

Responsible: Sofie Wahlström
Assignment: Unloading daytime
Avdelning: HUB – Production – Pallets
Process: Arrivals daytime
Updated: 2019-10-22
5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain om
Equipment

  -  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket, if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

  -  Communication equipment: ICOM-walkie-talkie is available, if needed. The walkie-talkie is charged at the office.

  -  Computers: One production computer between chutes 26 and 28. Four computers for all crew to use are located at the main office.

  -  Forklift computer: Two tablets at the Team leader office. For login information, see figure 1.

  -  PDA: Use the one marked with Pallet Handler. Charged at the main office.

  -  Forklift: Counterbalance forklift marked with Pallet distributor 2, alternatively pallet truck marked with Cage driving 1, both located at the forklift recharge station. Replacement batteries are available if required; change the battery before reaching 20%. Clean the truck from garbage after production. Water the truck if the battery power exceeds 90%. After production, the forklift must be charged. If the forklift is broken, notify Pontus Nero of which type of damage have occurred and attach a damage-flag to the forklift to indicate that the forklift is damaged and may not be used. Ask your supervisor which forklift to use if the primary forklift is unavailable.

  -  Other equipment: ballpoint pen, knife, marker, measuring tape.

  -  If equipment is missing contact your supervisor.

Workstation

The primary workstation is at gates 13-55 on the terminal.

  •  Open the doors on the terminal by placing your card against the card reader and enter your password if necessary.

  •  During production, you will need to sign shipping notes to drivers; Custom papers to Oslo1 NOO-JKG2 are to be left in the Customs area, Dangerous goods papers to be handed to your supervisor, the remaining paper is thrown into the appropriate trash bin.

  •  If garbage from pallets are located on the terminal floor, these must be removed and placed between the gatehouses and preferably in the trash baskets. The wheels of the forklifts will wear hard if we do not remove them.

  •  The pallets may only be placed in each specific area due to the risk of poor visibility.

Cleaning

After production, clean the truck used during the work shift. Cleaning agents are available.
