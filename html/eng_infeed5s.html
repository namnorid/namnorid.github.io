Infeed 5S

Responsible: Daniel Namnori
Assignment: Package feeder
Section: HUB – Production – Packages
Process: Package sorting
Updated: 2019-10-29
5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain om
Equipment

-  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

-  Communication equipment: Non available.

-  Computers: Four computers for all crew to use are located at the office.

-  Forklift: Non available.

-  Other equipment: Marker and knife (figure 1.)

-  If equipment is missing, contact Team leader.

Workstation

The primary workstation is in front of the sorting machine at the terminal across gates 51-56, see SOP.

1.Make sure not to have your feet in the way of the forklift on the wrong side of the impact protection (fig. 2.)

2.Keep the floor free of debris.

3.The plastic compressor is located between infeed 1 and 2 (fig. 3). The plastic waste compressor is to be packed continuously during the night. An EMB cage (parcels too big for the sorter, see SOP) is located at each feeding, to be placed with the opening towards the sorter. Tape container is on the right of each infeed (fig 4.)

Cleaning

After production, you clean the area you have received on the list of cleaning positions. Cleaning zones may vary. Always check the cleaning list at the office.

Daily cleaning on cleaning zone 4 (fig. 5) 'Infeed':

1.Sweep the entire area.

2.Drive all corrugated cardboard and broken pallets to the stand opposite the truck load.

3.Empty plastic from the triangle in the plastic compressor.

4.Empty the plastic compressor (to be done every night.)

6.Clean on top of the plastic compressor.
