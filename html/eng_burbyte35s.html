Cage driving 3 5S

Responsible: Daniel Namnori
Assignment: Package sorting
Section: HUB – Production – Sorting
Process: Pallet wrapping - Loading
Updated: 2019-10-22
5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain
Equipment

  -  Clothing: Ordinary Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

-  Communication equipment: Non available.

-  Computers: Four computers for general use in the office.

  -  Forklift: High lift forklift marked with Cage driving 3, this is located at the forklift recharge station. Replacement batteries are available if required. Make sure to change the battery prior reaching 20%. Clean the truck from garbage after production. Water the truck if battery power exceeds 90%. After production, the forklift must be charged. If the forklift is broken, notify Niclas Klahr of which type of damage have occurred and attach a damage-flag to the forklift to indicate that the forklift is damaged and may not be used if the damage is severe. Ask Team Leader which forklift to use if the primary forklift is unavailable.

-  Other equipment: ballpoint pen, knife, marker, staple gun (Figure 2.)

-  If equipment is missing contact Team leader.

Workstation

The primary workstation is around the sorting machine. Open the doors on the terminal by placing your card against the card reader and enter your password if necessary. If garbage from pallets is located on the terminal floor, these must be removed and placed between the gatehouses and preferably in the trash baskets, the wheels of the forklifts will wear hard if we do not remove them.

Städning

After production, you clean the area you have received on the list of cleaning positions. Cleaning zones may vary; always check the cleaning list.
Daily cleaning at cleaning zone 8 (fig. 1) ’Longside, departures + gatehouses’:

1.  Remove all pallets and cages from the area.

2.  Sweep out of the sorting machine.

3.  Clean the area between the gates and gatehouses from pallet debris and paper.

4.  Sweep out of the gates.

2.  Drive cages to the track.

3.  Fill the cage compartment with cages.

4.  Drive the yellow bin together with zone 1; Gather the garbage heaps around the sorter and empty the bucket in the compactor outside gate 12.
