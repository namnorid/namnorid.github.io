    Sorter long side 5S

    Responsible: Daniel Namnori
    Assignment: Chutes sorting
    Avdelning: HUB – Production – Packages
    Process: Package sorting
    Updated: 2019-09-04
    5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain om
    Equipment

    -  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

    -  Communication equipment: None available.

    -  Computers: Four computers for all crew to use are located at the office.

    -  Forklift: None.

    -  Other equipment: ballpoint pen, knife, marker (fig. 2.)

    -  If equipment is missing contact Team leader.

    Workstation

    The primary workstation is located at the sorting machine at the terminal, see SOP. Cages are placed next to the chutes in marked locations with opening outwards. The rear cages must be closed due to lack of space. Corrugated paper containers are used for Finland packages. Cage direction flags for all directions can be found on the right side of the EMB. Make sure not to walk out into the forklift aisle.

    Cleaning

    After production, make sure the compartments between the chutes are filled with cages, for a smooth production the following day. No pallets deployed on the short side; these should be placed next to the ADR area (fig. 3.) Clean the area you have received on the list of cleaning positions. The cleaning zones may vary, always check the cleaning list.
    Daily cleaning on cleaning zone 13 (fig. 1) 'Clean the kitchen + office area':

    1.  Clean the main office and bring cup box out to the kitchen.

    2.  Start the dishwasher for all trays; cutlery, plates, glasses and mugs.

    3.  Set the clean disk to their respective locations.

    4.  Get rid of debris and scraps.

    5.  Wipe off sinks and all tables, and set up chairs.
