Daytime 1 SOP

Responsible: Sofie Wahlström
Assignment: Unloading daytime
Section: HUB – Production – Pallets
Process: Arrivals daytime
Updated: 2019-10-24
SOP: Standard Operating Procedure
Start

Begin by collecting the equipment specified in the 5S. Do not forget the work phone (”the HUB phone”.)

Ordinarie arbete

The main task is to unload arriving cars, scan pallets (product code 0336), and drive scanned pallets to the respective area. During high season, extra cars may be added during the day; mostly mornings and export goods (NO, FI, DK), but also domestic cars. In these cases, the traffic department calls the HUB phone.

During the day, if there is time

•      Check the forklifts; clean and put on charge.

•      Help the caretaker, Paul, with damaged goods.

•      Clean the terminal.

Remaining goods

1.      Review remaining goods from night production and estimate time and number. PDAs and current login details are located at the walkie talk station at the main office.

2.      In the PDA: Goods → General deviations → Reason: 41 Delayed → J Delivered / reforwarded by first possible dispatch → leave Notes empty → scan the barcode on the remaining goods.

•      Start by PDA-scanning remaining EMB goods and work your way around the terminal.

3.      Fill in the KPI sheet (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Jönköping\PR Hub\PR Pslplanering\KPI\KPI 2018 Produktion Natt), and the Delayed Goods sheet (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Jönköping\PR Hub\PR Pslplanering\KPI).

CMR:s for Finland

1.      Print CMR for Finland and prepare for the first departure (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Lastlistor\Sverige\Finland)

•      2 copies to the driver and 1 copy to us.

2.      Print the tabs: CMR Turku, CMR Vantaa (BRING FIN) and CMR POSTI. 3 copies of each.

•      Do not forget to change the date on all three and enter the plomb number. Plombs are available at the office.

•      No CMR for Turku on Fridays.

3.      Create new traffic report from template with today's date (F:\Bring\Parcels\PR Production\PR Jönköping\PR Hub\Nattrapport\Trafikrapport 2019\P&L …)

•      Fill in arriving cars with arrival time and number of pallet places.

The emails (terminal.natt@bring.com)

1.      Check the email around 09:30 and read the email from the customs department; if the goods from the Norway trucks are approved to leave the customs warehouse (if you have any questions please call: 0046 765 271 784.)

a.      Review which shipments have been approved.

b.      Compare the four-digit arrival number, from the email, with the goods so that the right goods are handled.

c.      Drive approved goods with product code 0342 to gate 14, for further loading to DHL; with departure between 10:30 and 11:30.

i. Note, approved goods from the customs department to DHL must be PDA scanned.
ii. Press Unloading → Received at hub → scan the barcode, press Ok → press Ok.
iii. The current login is available at the main office at the walkietalkie station.

d.      Drive approved 0332 and 0336 to the split square, and the pallet square in the southern hall.

2.      Print the email and all attached papers for ”LQ-gods till SEJ-FIH 1”

•      Have the papers ready for the departure for the first Finland car, SEJ-FIH 1.

At low season or if there is time available: 12:30-13:30 - Finland sorting:

1.      Build the short side with used cardboard containers from the return department. Chute 25, 26: FIA 4. Chute 24: FIA 3. Chute 23: FIA 2. Chute 21, 22: FIA 1.

•      During the winter months, the Tura (customer) goods are picked up from the Northern Hall. In the summer, Tura unloads directly inside the Southern Hall.

2.      Help with sorting; either put up packages on the sorting path or receive sorted packages on the short side and add the associated cardboard containers.

3.      Unload STO-JKP 1 (returning Finland’s truck) and DKE-SEJ 2 in gate 51–55. These two cars are prioritized before you help with the sorting of Finland.

4.      4. Around 14:30, drive to the customs area and scan the approved 0336 goods with the truck pc. N.B. that do not scan stopped goods (red sticky note); should remain in the customs area. Drive the scanned 0336 to the respective gate / area and split goods to the split square.

Notices

1.      See current arriving cars in the traffic report, tab Daytime; for arriving cars until 18:00. The remainder is under the Inrikes Inboundtab.

2.      Aim to take a break at 11am. After the break, go through and plan the day with your supervisor, and inform about the morning's production.

•      At low season or if there is time, check with RK (return department) if they need help driving out pallets, or take time to clean your truck.

3.      Should time be available, talk to your supervisor.

End of production

Clean the forklift and your workstation after the work shift is complete.
