Responsible: Robert Fransson
Assignment: Parcel sorting
Section: HUB – Production – Packages
Process: Sorting of Export parcels
Updated: 2019-09-17
5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain om
Equipment

-  Clothes: Ordinary Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

-  Communications equipment: Non available.

-  Computers: Four computers for general use in the office.

-  OE computer: A desktop computer for CBM registrations, work in LM, and printouts of shipping letters (CN37, CP87, bag label); located on the OE workstation.

-  LMK printers: Printer E3 used when printing the package number that is attached to the invoices. Printer Prod-IT00257 is used with the CBM computer. Printer P139106226092 is used for printing of shipping notes or for copying invoices. Printer P106226121 is used for printing cage flags, CMRs and other things that are printed in quantities. Empty A4 sheets can be found in our material warehouse outside the main office. Small size label rolls are inside the Team leader’s office; Ask Team leaders about these when refilling is needed.

-  Cage flags: OE flags for labeling of pallets and cages is to the left of the CBM printer.

-  Forklift: Forklift marked with OE which stands at the regular truck loading. Replacement batteries are available if needed. Change before 20 % of battery power remains. Clean the forklift from general debris after production. Water the forklift if the battery power exceeds 90 %. After production, the forklift should be put on charge. If the forklift is broken, inform Niclas Klahr about the type of problem and put a note on the forklift that it is damaged and must not be used. Ask the Team leader about which forklift to use instead.

-  Office supplies: Bring tape, office tape, paperclips, stickers, printer paper, and C5 plastic pockets. These are in the filing cabinet.

-  Other equipment: Marker, knife, stapler (fig. 2.)

-  If equipment is missing contact your Team leader.

Workstation

The primary workstation is located at between chutes 26 and 27 at the terminal. The floor surfaces must be free of debris. The trash can is located under the OE desk. If you drive a forklift inside the OE area, you must be very careful. Personal equipment must not be placed at the workstation to facilitate rotations of personnel during production. The desktop surface should always be free of debris and items that are not used daily.

Cleaning

After production, you clean the area you have been assigned on the Cleaning position list. Cleaning zones may vary, always check the cleaning list.
Daily cleaning on cleaning zone 2 (fig. 1) ’OE’:

1.  The sweep the OE area.

2.  Put the scanners on charge.

3.  Empty the bins.

4.  Fill with new pallets and Norwegian cages.

5.  Print flag for shelf at EMB.

6.  Build up the track with new corrugated cardboard collars.

7.  Sweep the floor at chute 1.

8.  Drive away the finished goods.

Big cleaning every month:

1.  Wipe workspaces and computer screen.

2.  Clean the entire floor area, including under the sorting path.
