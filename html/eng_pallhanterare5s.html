Pallet handler 5S

Responsible: Daniel Namnori
Assignment: Arrival management
Section: HUB – Production – Pallets
Process: Arrival register arriving pallet
Updated: 2019-10-30
5S: Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain om
Equipment

-  Clothing: Regular Bring uniform. Extra warm layer and jacket if needed. Safety gloves and safety shoes.

-  Communication equipment: Non available.

-  Computers: Four computers for all crew to use at the main office.

-  Truck PC: Two truck PC’s charging at the Team leader office. Use the one marked with ’Pallet handler’.

-  PDA: Marked with ’Pallet handler’. Charged at the main office.

-  Forklift: Forklift labeled with Pallet handler; located at the regular charging area. Exchange batteries are available if required. Make sure to change the battery prior reaching 20 %. Clean the forklift from general garbage after production. Water the forklift if the battery power level exceeds 90 %. After production, the forklift must be charged. If the forklift is broken, please inform Niclas Klahr who oversees the forklifts, and mark the forklift with information that the forklift is not to be used. Ask nearest Team leader which forklift to be used temporarily until the regular forklift fully function again.

-  Other equipment: Ballpoint pen, knife, marker, label rolls, measuring tape (fig. 1), and a label roll (fig. 2.)

-  If equipment is missing, contact your Team leader.

Workstation

The primary workstation is between gates 45-50 and the sorting machine, and in front of gates 56, see fig. 4. And pallets on the transit area in the northern hall (fig. 5.)

Cleaning

After production, clean the area you have been assigned to on the Cleaning position list. Cleaning zones may vary; always check the cleaning list.
Daily cleaning on cleaning zone 1 (fig. 3) ’Shortside, arrivals + gatehouses’:

1.  Drive away all goods from the track to the respective area infront of the gates.

2.  Drive empty pallets to the area infront of gate 12. Stack the pallets neatly.

3.  Drive empty cages from the arriving side to the compartments between the sorting machine's chutes.

4.  Cleaning of the area between the gates and gatehouses from pallet debris and paper; sweep it out of the gates.

5.  Sweep the area by the chutes.

6.  Fill the compartments with cages.

7.  Help others with their respective cleaning areas.

8.  Drive the yellow bin together with zone 8; Gather the garbage heaps around the sorter and empty the bucket in the compactor outside port 12.

Big cleaning every month: Cleaning behind the yellow guardrails at "Long Jan."
